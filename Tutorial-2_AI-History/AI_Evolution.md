## History of AI: 1950s → Present

**1950s: The Birth**
- **1950**: Alan Turing publishes "Computing Machinery and Intelligence" + Turing Test
- **1956**: Dartmouth Conference - term "Artificial Intelligence" coined by John McCarthy. This is considered AI's birth year
- Goal: Make machines that can "think" like humans

**1960s-1970s: Early Optimism → AI Winter 1**  
- ELIZA chatbot, early neural networks, symbolic AI
- Funding cuts due to overpromised results. Research slowed down

**1980s-1990s: Expert Systems + AI Winter 2**
- Expert systems like MYCIN for medical diagnosis
- **1997**: IBM Deep Blue beats chess champion Garry Kasparov

**2000s: Machine Learning Rise**
- More data + computing power = better algorithms
- **2011**: IBM Watson wins Jeopardy
- **2012**: AlexNet wins ImageNet - deep learning breakthrough

**2010s-Present: Deep Learning Era**
- Neural networks with many layers dominate
- **2016**: AlphaGo beats Go champion Lee Sedol
- **2022-Present**: Large Language Models go mainstream

## Evolution of Generative AI

Generative AI = AI that creates new content vs just analyzing data

1. **Rule-based generation 1950s-1980s**: Hand-coded grammar rules. ELIZA was early example
2. **Statistical models 1990s-2010s**: N-grams, HMMs. Predict next word based on probability
3. **Neural generation 2014-2017**: RNNs, LSTMs. GANs introduced in 2014 for images
4. **Transformers 2017-Present**: "Attention is All You Need" paper. Foundation for ChatGPT, DALL-E, Midjourney
5. **LLMs + Diffusion 2020-Present**: GPT-3, GPT-4, Stable Diffusion. Text, image, code, audio generation at human-level quality

## Applications

**Today AI is used in:**
1. **NLP**: Chatbots, translation, summarization, writing assistants
2. **Computer Vision**: Face recognition, medical imaging, self-driving cars  
3. **Generative Media**: Text-to-image, music generation, video synthesis
4. **Healthcare**: Drug discovery, disease diagnosis, personalized treatment
5. **Finance**: Fraud detection, algorithmic trading, risk assessment
6. **Education**: Personalized tutoring, auto-grading, content creation
7. **Science**: Protein folding with AlphaFold, climate modeling

**Future direction**: More multimodal AI, AI agents that can take actions, and better alignment with human values.
