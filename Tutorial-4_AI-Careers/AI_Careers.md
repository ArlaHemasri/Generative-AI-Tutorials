# 📘 Tutorial Task 4: AI Career Explorer

## Problem Statement
Identify and document emerging career opportunities in Generative AI.

## 1. Why GenAI Careers Are Booming - 2026
After ChatGPT, companies need people who can build, use, and govern AI. Unlike old ML jobs that needed PhDs, GenAI created roles for students, developers, and non-coders too.

## 2. Top Emerging Careers in Generative AI

### 1. Prompt Engineer
**Role**: Designs, tests, and optimizes prompts to get accurate outputs from LLMs like ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude.
**Why it’s emerging**: Companies realized "good prompting = 10x better AI results". No coding needed initially.
**Key Skills**: LLM behavior understanding, creativity, clear communication, Python basics
**Tools**: OpenAI API, LangChain, PromptLayer, Claude
**Salary India**: ₹8-25 LPA | Fresher: ₹6-10 LPA
**Example Task**: Make ChatGPT act like a "Nuzvid tourism guide" with perfect outputs every time

### 2. AI Engineer / LLM Engineer  
**Role**: Builds and deploys AI systems. Fine-tunes models, creates RAG apps, integrates APIs.
**Why it’s emerging**: Every app now needs "AI features". Someone must connect ChatGPT to company data.
**Key Skills**: Python, PyTorch/TensorFlow, APIs, Vector Databases, Docker
**Tools**: Hugging Face, LangChain, FAISS, Pinecone, Streamlit
**Salary India**: ₹15-40 LPA | Fresher: ₹8-15 LPA
**Example Task**: Build a chatbot that answers questions from your college PDF notes

### 3. Data Scientist - GenAI Focus
**Role**: Analyzes data to train/evaluate AI models. Checks if AI outputs are correct + unbiased.
**Why it’s emerging**: GenAI needs massive data + evaluation. "Garbage in = garbage out".
**Key Skills**: Statistics, Python, SQL, Data visualization, Model evaluation
**Tools**: Pandas, NumPy, Weights&Biases, MLflow, SQL
**Salary India**: ₹12-35 LPA | Fresher: ₹7-12 LPA
**Example Task**: Test if DALL·E generates biased images and fix the training data

### 4. AI Researcher
**Role**: Develops new AI algorithms, models, and techniques. Publishes papers.
**Why it’s emerging**: Models like GPT-5, Llama 4 need new research in efficiency, safety, reasoning.
**Key Skills**: Deep Learning, Math/Linear Algebra, Research papers, PyTorch
**Tools**: PyTorch, JAX, Google Colab, ArXiv, Weights&Biases
**Salary India**: ₹20-60 LPA | Academia + Industry labs
**Works at**: OpenAI, Google DeepMind, Meta AI, IITs, Microsoft Research

### 5. AI Product Manager - Bonus Role
**Role**: Decides what GenAI features to build. Talks to users + engineers.
**Why it’s emerging**: Tech teams can build AI, but PMs decide "should we build this for users?"
**Key Skills**: Product thinking, User research, LLM use cases, No heavy coding
**Tools**: ChatGPT, Notion AI, Figma AI, Jira
**Salary India**: ₹20-50 LPA
